Pasta Salad

Description

This pasta salad with pepperoni, tomatoes, and provolone cheese is the best I've ever eaten, and people request it frequently. It's a very easy, light-tasting side dish for a picnic or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package fusilli (spiral) pasta
  • 3 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 lb provolone cheese, cubed
  • 1/2 lb salami, cubed
  • 1/4 lb sliced pepperoni, cut in half
  • 1 large green bell pepper, cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 1 (10 oz) can black olives, drained
  • 1 (4 oz) jar pimientos, drained
  • 1 (8 oz) bottle Italian salad dressing

Steps

  1. Mise en place
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook fusillii> in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes. Drain.
  3. Combine fusilli with tomatoes, cheese, salami, pepperoni, green pepper, olives, and pimentos in a large bowl. Pour in salad dressing; toss to coat.
  4. Enjoy!
