Apricot chicken

Description

Make this easy, flavorful apricot chicken in the oven, slow cooker, pressure cooker, or even on the grill. You just canâ€™t go wrong with this recipe!

Ingredients

  • 3 chicken breasts
  • 1 cup apricot preserves
  • 1 cup Catalina salad dressing (or Russian dressing)
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 ounces dried onion soup mix

Steps

  1. Mise en place
  2. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  3. Cut chicken breasts in half or into serving-sized portions.
  4. In a medium-sized bowl, combine apricot preserves, Catalina dressing, onion soup mix, and Dijon mustard. Mix well.
  5. Place chicken in a greased 9x13 baking dish. Pour apricot mixture evenly over chicken and bake 40-45 minutes or until chicken is cooked through.
  6. Serve with greens.
